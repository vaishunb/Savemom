Instruction

  •  1. These yoga postures have been checked by yoga teachers as safe for you to try from 14 weeks of pregnancy.

  •  2. If any of the positions or stretches become uncomfortable for you, stop doing them.

  •  3. It's always a good idea to talk to your doctor or healthcare professional before starting any form of exercise while you're pregnant, especially if it's a type you haven't done before.
    •


Step 1:


Start by sitting on a chair with your feet placed comfortably and firmly on the ground.

Step 2:


Breathe in and gently place your right leg on your left thigh.
Settle your right ankle comfortably on your left knee in this crossover position.

Step 3:


Now, place your hands on your bent right leg with your palms facing upwards.
Adjust your legs comfortably and slowly bend forward with your arms slipping forward.
Exhale slowly while bending forward, keeping neck and spine in line.
Feel the stretch in your spine, right hip and groin area.
Hold the position for a few breaths.
Inhale and slowly come back to an upright position.
Slowly place your right leg back on the ground, exhale and sit back and relax in the chair.
Then repeat on the left side.

Benefits

Opens up your hip joints
Stretches the muscles in your lower pelvic area.
Thought to be a good stress reliever.


Tips

Keep your movements slow and controlled.
Always return gradually to your starting position.
